Who We Are

SGIPC is a student-led programming community focused on problem solving, algorithmic thinking, and team collaboration. We provide structured pathways for beginners, advanced training for contestants, and opportunities to represent our university in regional and international contests.

Our Mission

Enable students to become confident competitive programmers by combining regular contests, practical workshops, and personalized guidance from experienced mentors.

Our Vision

Build one of the most active university programming ecosystems where innovation, discipline, and consistency lead to strong national and global performances.

What We Offer

Weekly Practice Contests

Sharpen speed and accuracy with frequent problem-solving sessions.

Focused Bootcamps

Dive deep into data structures, graphs, dynamic programming, and more.

Team Selection Pipeline

Transparent progress path for representing SGIPC in external contests.
