About This Workshop

Take your competitive programming skills to the next level with our Advanced Graph Theory Bootcamp!

This 3-day intensive workshop drops you right into the deep end of algorithm design, specifically tailored for upcoming regional and national programming competitions.

Curriculum & Prerequisites

  • Day 1: Shortest Paths: Advanced Dijkstra techniques, Bellman-Ford, and Floyd-Warshall.
  • Day 2: Spanning Trees and Articulation Points: Prim's algorithm, Kruskal's, Tarjan's algorithm.
  • Day 3: Heavy-Light Decomposition and Network Flow (Max Flow Min Cut).
  • Prerequisite 1: Strong understanding of basic graph traversal (BFS, DFS).
  • Prerequisite 2: Familiarity with C++ STL data structures.
